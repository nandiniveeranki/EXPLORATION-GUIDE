Agriculture in India

India is one of the world's largest agricultural economies. Various regions specialize in different crops:

  • Punjab & Haryana – Renowned for wheat and rice production.
  • Andhra Pradesh – Known for rice, cotton, and chili cultivation.
  • Maharashtra – Major producer of sugarcane and cotton.
