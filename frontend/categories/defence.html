The Officers Training Academy (OTA), with campuses in Chennai (Tamil Nadu) and Gaya (Bihar), is a premier institution of the Indian Army that trains men and women for Short Service Commission (SSC). The Northern Command thus serves as the frontline shield of India, ensuring national security against external aggression and internal threats in one of the most challenging geographies in the world.

INS Kadamba, located at Karwar in Karnataka, is India‚Äôs premier naval base and the largest naval base in Asia, developed under the ambitious Project Seabird to strengthen the Indian Navy‚Äôs operational reach and maritime security. Indian Naval Academy (INA), Ezhimala, Kerala.

The Indian Naval Academy (INA), Ezhimala, Kerala, established in 2009, is the premier training institution of the Indian Navy and the largest naval academy in Asia, designed to shape future naval officers into leaders of character, competence, and courage. The Western Naval Command (WNC), headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, is one of the Indian Navy‚Äôs three operational commands and serves as the principal fleet command for the Arabian Sea, overseeing India‚Äôs western maritime front. INS Hansa, located near Dabolim Airport in Goa, is one of the Indian Navy‚Äôs premier naval aviation bases, established in 1961 shortly after Goa‚Äôs integration into India. Hindon Air Force Station, located in Ghaziabad near Delhi, is one of the largest airbases in Asia and a key strategic installation of the Indian Air Force (IAF). Air Force Academy (AFA), Dundigal, Hyderabad

The Air Force Academy (AFA), Dundigal, Hyderabad, established in 1969, is the premier training institution of the Indian Air Force (IAF), responsible for shaping future officers in flying, ground duties, and technical branches.
